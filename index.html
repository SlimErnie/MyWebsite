About me

Hi, my name is Arne Wendler. Welcome to my personal website.

Here you will find more information about what drives me, my background and also how to get in touch with me.

Traffic is all around us. The way we get from our home to work or to different freetime activities. How groceries get to the supermarket, or how data travels from one point of the world to another in a matter of seconds. Often we take these forms of travelling/traffic for granted and don't see what is all working behind that. From my early days on traffic, especially cars and airplanes always fascinated me. Probably that's the reason why my first dreamjob was to become a pilot :D

Growing up, because pilots weren't needed that much, my focus had to shift to something else in the buzzing world of traffic.

In my quest I found TU Dresden and especially the Transportation economics program which helped me understand the world of traffic firstly from an economists point of view but also offered insights into ecological and psychological views on traffic.

Armed with that knowledge FDTech - a mobility solutions provider for the future - offered me an Internship and a following working student employment.

Climate change, the demographic change, road safety, equality and other factors force us to think different about traffic and traffic solutions than we did before. Taking advantage of the modern technologies we have at our hands we must face these new challenges and find solutions to take care of the wellbeing of our planet, our fellow men and women and ourselves.

Being a part of the new change in Traffic!
